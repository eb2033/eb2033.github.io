> about

Background

I am a Russian national who grew up living in Ireland. I am currently studying omputer networks and cybersecurity in the Technological university of the Shannon. I love to experiment with linux environments and security tooling.

Skills & Technologies

My main languages are Python, YML and PHP with brief exposure to SQL, C++ and Javascript. I frequently use the docker engine and Docker Compose on my homelab environments and am planning to expand that with Ansible and Terraform.

Philosophy

I believe in secure architecture design and defense in depth. When a system is inevitably compromised in one way or another, the goal isn't to prevent every intrusion—it's to ensure that compromise is detected quickly, contained effectively, and recoverable without catastrophe. Security isn't a wall you build once; it's a posture you maintain: layered controls, strong visibility, and the assumption that any single control can fail.
